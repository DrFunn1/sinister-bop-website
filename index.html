JOY CIRCUIT

Joy Circuit is a popular, all original band from the Northeast Ohio USA eighties new wave scene.

Joy Circuit explores the connections between man and machine with their blend of synthesized soundscapes, big rhythm and sonorous vocals. They create a synthesized wall of sound reminiscent of Gary Numan and a darker Duran Duran. A unique, custom built light show with rear projection creates an original, visually interesting live show experience.

GRAFF PLASTIC

Graff Plastic is an experimental music collaborative.

Graff Plastic is a group of out of the box thinkers, using sounds in their environment, building instruments when dissatisfied with what is available, blending synthesized electronics, and found items for percussion. Performances are often spontaneous and single takes, followed by cries of "Genius!"
